# Bundled content & licenses

## Face detection (reading-presence check)

- **Files:** `resources/models/blaze_face_short_range.tflite`,
  `resources/models/wasm/` (MediaPipe tasks-vision runtime), plus the
  `@mediapipe/tasks-vision` npm package bundled into the renderer.
- **Source:** Google MediaPipe (BlazeFace short-range face detector).
- **License:** Apache License 2.0. Detection runs entirely on-device; no
  image or video ever leaves the machine.

## Scripture sources & licenses

All scripture text is bundled locally in `resources/scripture/` (generated by
`scripts/fetch-scripture.mjs` at build time; the app itself never makes
network requests).

## Bible — King James Version (KJV)

- **File:** `resources/scripture/bible-kjv.json`
- **Source:** getbible.net v2 API export of the King James Version
  (66 books, 31,102 verses).
- **License:** The KJV text (1611/1769) is in the public domain in the
  United States and most of the world. (In the UK, printed reproduction is
  governed by perpetual Crown letters patent; bundled digital personal use
  as here is standard practice.) getbible.net's packaging metadata is GPL;
  the underlying scripture text itself carries no copyright.

## Quran — Arabic text

- **File:** `resources/scripture/quran.json` (`ar` fields)
- **Source:** Tanzil Project (tanzil.net), Quran text — simple script,
  6,236 ayas.
- **Attribution (required by Tanzil's terms of use):**

  > Quran text courtesy of Tanzil.net. This copy of the Quran text is
  > carefully produced, highly verified and continuously monitored by a
  > group of specialists at Tanzil Project.

  Per Tanzil's terms, the text is used unmodified and this notice is
  retained. See <https://tanzil.net/docs/text_license>

## Quran — English translation

- **File:** `resources/scripture/quran.json` (`en` fields)
- **Translation:** *The Meaning of the Glorious Koran*, Marmaduke Pickthall
  (1930), as distributed by Tanzil.
- **License:** Public domain — published 1930 (US public domain since
  January 1, 2026); author died 1936 (life+70 worldwide since 2007).
